resin bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Megachile heriadiformis

    

Size
female 10 mm; male 9 mm

Identifying Characteristics
Females may be identified by their long, narrow mandibles in combination with the broken hair bands on T2 and T3. Males have the same broken hair bands and unmodified fore tarsi.

Distinctive Features
Only this species and M. leucopyga have similar metasomal hair patterns and females are readily distinguished by their head shape and males by their black femora.
