resin bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Megachile semiluctuosa

    

Size
female 16 mm; male 12 to 13 mm

Identifying Characteristics
A very large black species with white hair bands on T1 and T2 (broken in male) and lateral white hair on T3.

Distinctive Features
Females have a distinctive short clypeus with the underside exposed and only 3 teeth on the mandibles. Males have expanded fore tarsi and the final antennal segment is flattened and expanded. These features, together with the white hair pattern make it easy to distinguish this species from either M. lucidiventris or M. punctata.
