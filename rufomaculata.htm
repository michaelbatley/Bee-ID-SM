resin bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Megachile (Austrochile) rufomaculata

    

Size
female 10 mm; male 9 mm

Identifying Characteristics
Females have large projections from the face. Males have orange-brown forelegs and chesnut patches along the sides of the metasoma.

Distinctive Features
No other female in the area has similar projections. Males have no orange hair, only orange-brown integument on the metasoma.
