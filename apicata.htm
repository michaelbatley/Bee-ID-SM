resin bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Megachile apicata

    

Size
female 7 mm; male 6 mm

Identifying Characteristics
Very small species: female clypeus has two small teeth, exoskeleton orange at end of abdomen; male with yellow mandibles and row of teeth on S6.

Distinctive Features
The grooves across the front edges of abdominal segments 2 to 4 carry short, highly branched white hairs. The only other species in the area with similar hair in the grooves is M. hackeri.
