leafcutter bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Megachile (Eutricharea) macularis

    

Size
female 11 mm; male 10 mm

Identifying Characteristics
A robust species with thick white hair bands and white or pale gold hair on the face and end of the abdomen.

Distinctive Features
Similar to M. maculariformis, but without orange hair on the face or abdomen. The expanded forelegs of the male have no bulbous orange protrusion.
