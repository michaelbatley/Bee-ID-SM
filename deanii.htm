resin bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Megachile (Rhodomegachile) deanii

    

Size
female 8 mm; male 7 mm

Identifying Characteristics
Small species with abdomen almost entirely orange-brown with negligible tomentum (hair).

Distinctive Features
The two other species with an orange abdomen have orange hair over a dark integument (chitinous exoskeleton), unlike this species. Males may be distinguished from females by the rounded tip of the abdomen, the absence of the set of pollen-carrying hairs (scopa) under the abdomen and the longer antennae.
The species Megachile micrerythrura is similar in size and appearance but has not been found near Sydney.
