resin bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Megachile (Hackeriapis) oblonga

    

Size
female 9.5 mm; male 8 mm

Identifying Characteristics
A moderate-sized species with orange hair forming a spot on the end of the abdomen. Females have two complete white hair bands, but on males the bands are broken in the middle.

Distinctive Features
Differs from M. rhodura by the orange hair on the abdomen which does not reach the sides in M. oblonga.
