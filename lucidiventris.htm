DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Megachile lucidiventris

    

Size
female 14 mm; male 11 mm

Identifying Characteristics
Large black species with dark wings and long white hair with long white hair completely covering first segment of metasoma.

Distinctive Features
Similar to M. punctata but without white spots on thorax, and to M. semiluctuosa but without white hair on T2 and T3.
